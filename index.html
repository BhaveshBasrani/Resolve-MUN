About the Conference

WHERE DIPLOMACY
MEETS AMBITION

Resolve MUN is not just a conference — it is an arena where the next generation of global leaders converge to debate, deliberate, and drive change. Built on the pillars of intellectual rigor and diplomatic excellence, Resolve MUN challenges delegates to engage with the world's most pressing issues.

From crisis committees that demand split-second decisions to general assemblies that require sweeping coalition-building, every moment at Resolve MUN is designed to forge leaders of consequence.

Conference Details

THE ESSENTIALS

01

Date & Duration

Three intensive days of diplomacy, debate, and deliberation. Precise dates to be announced — mark your calendar.

02

Venue

A world-class conference facility befitting the calibre of debate. Full venue details coming soon.

03

Delegate Count

Limited to 500 delegates across 8 specialized committees, ensuring high-quality debate and meaningful participation.

04

Awards

Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, Verbal Commendation, and Best Position Paper awards across all committees.

05

Registration Fee

Tiered registration available. Early bird pricing and institutional group rates available for schools and universities.

06

Social Night

After a day of intense debate, delegates come together for an exclusive evening social on Day 2 — an opportunity to connect, celebrate, and build friendships that extend far beyond the conference.
